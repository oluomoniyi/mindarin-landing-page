Confucius was never confused, but what about you?

Join the unique way to learn Chinese Mandarin characters with mind maps that focus on confusion first!

Learning Mandarin can certainly be confusing and especially the characters when there are over 50,000 of them!

Dictionaries will have 20,000 or so, an educated Chinese person would know around 8,000 and you need to know around 3000 to be able to read a newspaper.

What is Mindarin App?

Mindarin App helps you to structure your learning of Chinese Mandarin language focusing on confusion first. It groups similar looking Chinese characters for you and introduces the basic character radicals through an easy to follow story!

Who is this for?

Beginners and advanced learners, HSK students all can benefit from this unique way of ‘learning to learn’ to memorize Chinese Mandarin characters.

When will it be launched?

Go live is planned for Chinese New Year in 2018. The beta site will be launched in August 2017 and regular developments will be made monthly.
